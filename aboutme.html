About me

Jan Waśko. About 170 cm tall. Born in Cracow. Nothing too interesting, right?

Welcome! My name is Jan Waśko. I may not be the tallest, but I am a very brave human being. I was not afraid to change the course of my career when I realised what was missing in the field of study I was pursuing. I was born in a second-largest city of Poland, Cracow, where I had a great opportunity to study Social Informatics at AGH University of Science and Technology. User Experience is EVERYWHERE, even in writing as you can see. This is why I am so passionate about it.

Despite that, I would not have learned about UX, if I had not made some mistakes in my life. I began my academic career as a student of Applied Computer Science. I have always felt connected to IT and I have acted as the first line of contact for my family whenever they had a problem with their devices. Yet, while studying ACS, in addition to having my ups and downs with mathematics and programming, I constantly felt something was missing for me in this area of study. Eventually, I realised there was not enough focus on the human part of the technical world. By changing my field of study to Social Informatics, I made the best possible decision for my future. Not only have I received a fresh look on a Human-Computer Interaction, but also experienced a lot of interesting and creative human-centered processes. I cannot imagine my future working career without being connected to the research of User Interfaces.
