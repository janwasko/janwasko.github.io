About me

Jan Waśko. About 170 cm tall. Born in Cracow. - Nothing too interesting, right?

Welcome! My name is Jan Waśko. Maybe I am not the tallest, but, despite that, I am a very brave human being. I had this great opportunity to be born in a large city (Cracow) where I could study Social Informatics at AGH University of Science and Technology. - User Experience is EVERYWHERE, even in writting as you can see. That is why I am so passionate about it.

However, to be honest, I would not have learned about UX if I had not made mistakes in my life. I started my academic career as a student of Applied Computer Science. I have always felt connected to IT as I was the first line of contact for my family whenever they had a problem with their devices. During the studies I had been having my ups and downs with mathematics and programming, but all the the time something has been missing. There was not enough focus on the human part of the technical world. Having changed my field of study to Social Informatics, I made the best decision. Not only have I received a fresh look on a Human-Computer Interaction, but also experienced a lot of interesting and creative human-centered processes. I cannot imagine my future working life without being connected to research or User Interfaces.
