MAPeco - Define

Based on our interviews, we created two personas. I managed to find a photo of one of them:

Our personas say:

  • 'I'm sure that other people do not sort out rubbish properly.'
  • 'A bigger group could make bigger changes.'
  • 'I have little to no idea on that happens to the rubbish after they are taken by a dustman.'

Our personas think that:

  • 'It's a mess.'
  • 'I'm alone with the problem of sorting out the rubbish.'
  • 'I'm curious if my actions are worth the effort.'

What our personas do is:

  • They see the problem of waste management in social media channels.
  • They sort out the rubbish according to their belief.
  • They try to reduce the amount of plastic bought from supermartkets.

Our personas feel:

  • Frustration.
  • Loneliness.
  • Little to no hope.

That was the moment we realized that our interviewees need a solution:

  • in order to find out what happens to their rubbish after they throw it away;
  • in order not to feel alone in taking care of the environment;
  • in order not to be forced to buy products in plastic packaging.
