MAPeco - Define

Based on our interviews, we created two personas (but only one is shown below):

Our personas say: 'I'm sure that other people do not sort out rubbish properly.', 'A bigger group could make bigger changes.', 'I have little to no idea on that happens to rubbish after they are taken by dustman.'.

Our personas think that: 'It's a mess.', 'I'm alone with the problem of sorting out the rubbish.', 'I'm curious if my actions are worth the effort.'.

What our personas do is: they see the problem of waste management in social media, they sort out the rubbish according to their belief, they try to reduce the amount of plastic bought from supermartkets.

Our personas feel: frustration, loneliness, little to no hope.

That was the moment we realized that our interviewees need a solution: to find out what happens to their rubbish after they throw it away, in order not to feel alone in taking care of the environment, in order not to be forced to buy products in plastic packaging and that will make them feel less lonely and better informed in the whole process of waste management.
