MuraLove - Gallery

Having empathized with people regarding their attitude to street art in the city, my team and I came to the point where we had a lot of information. This is why we used a card sorting method to divide gathered information into groups:

Once we were done with the sorting, we raised a question: 'How can we make Cracow a mural-friendly city?'. Then we began a brainstorming session:

We outdid ourselves and therefore there were a lot of ideas to be taken into consideration. How-Now-Wow Matrix was an excelent tool to focus on the most interesting solutions.

Alongside of MuraLove, you could find an idea of creating some gadgets (i.e. cups, T-shirts) with mural images on them or a mobile app, similar to the one where you catch Pokémons, but you would catch murals instead. We would not achieve that without the techniques mentioned above.
