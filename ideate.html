MAPeco - Ideate

During a little brainstorming session we generated four solutions that would heal the pain of our interviewees:

First of them was a mobile game that guided a player on how to sort out the rubbish properly. We hoped it would increase the awareness of proper recycling and make people feel less lonely as it contained high scores table.

Second one was also a mobile game, but this time it was focused mainly on the community. The game was about completing tasks connected to waste management, which granted achievements that you could share on your profile or send them to your friends.

Third solution's goal was to help with sorting out the rubbish as the app offered a barcode scanner which was meant to show where the product should be thrown away. It also informed about some reuse possibilities whenever it was possible.

Forth and the last one was a mobile app that showed zero-waste or at least less-waste places or events based on a user location.
