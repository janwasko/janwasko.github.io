Short description

A mobile app to help people with waste management. The project was carried out to get a credit for Design Thinking, User Interface Design and Service-Oriented Architecture seminars.

My team and I had to come up with an area of life where we saw a potential problem to solve. During a break between lecures we went to the supermarket and were like: 'Hey, people are using a lot of plastic bags. How about we try to change that?', but back then we did not know how to start. Next Design Thinking classes came to our rescue.
